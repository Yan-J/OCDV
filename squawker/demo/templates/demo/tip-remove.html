Clear Browsing History on iPhone

It is very important to clear your browsing history in order to ensure that your partner cannot track it. Below are step by step instructions on how to clear your history on an iPhone, iPod Touch or iPad. This will clear the list of websites that you have visited in Safari.

    1.Press Settings on the Home screen.

    2. Scroll down to Safari. Press Safari.

    3.Scroll down to Clear History and Website Data. Press Clear History and Website Data.

    4.Press Clear History and Website Data.
